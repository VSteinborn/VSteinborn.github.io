About Me

Hello, I am Victor Steinborn. I am a responsible AI specialist. I worked with companies that aim to build responsible AI products and automate tasks. My background is in research. I did my PhD in computer science at the LMU Munich and my MPhys in theoretical physics at the University of Edinbrugh. This website is a general personal site where I highlight some of my work. I hope you enjoy it, and if you would like to get in touch for a project, please do not hesitate to contact me.
